### Initial Problem Statement (Analytical Goal)

The primary objective of this project is to optimize the revenue strategy for New York City taxi drivers by identifying geographical zones with the highest tipping potential and profitability. 

In addition to identifying high-value positioning, the project includes a comprehensive analysis of the COVID-19 pandemic's impact on NYC taxi trends. By comparing data from 2019 (pre-pandemic) and 2020 (pandemic onset), the study evaluates whether the pandemic resulted in a reduction in tipping amounts and percentages.

By analyzing large-scale trip data, the project aims to pinpoint high-value areas and timeframes, allowing drivers to maximize their earnings through data-driven positioning in a post-pandemic landscape.

### Data Source
The analysis is based on the "New York Yellow Taxi Trip Data (2019-2020)" dataset.
Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/microize/newyork-yellow-taxi-trip-data-2020-2019
